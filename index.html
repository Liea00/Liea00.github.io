About

Hello, I’m Mei-Li Nieuwland / Liea [ li:’a ], currently doing freelance illustration and concept art from a small apartment-turned-studio in Amsterdam.


As an artist I'm self-taught, but I went to school for AI and anthropology — it's been a bit of a detour. Copious amounts of love go to science & tech, food, learning languages, games, and fashion, and these all fuse in my ambition to create things that are beautiful to look at in new and interesting ways.


I work 100% digitally using an Intuos 4 Wacom tablet, Photoshop CS5, and Easy Paint Tool SAI.

Clients: Hasbro • VPRO • Citizenhub • Noio • FreeToPlay
