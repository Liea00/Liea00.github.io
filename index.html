About

Hello, I’m Mei-Li Nieuwland, also going by the alias Liea [ lee-ya ] online. I'm doing freelance illustration from a small apartment-turned-studio in Amsterdam.


As an artist I'm self-taught, but my academic background is in artificial intelligence and cultural anthropology. What inspires me is a mix of science and technology, culture and society, psychology and the mind. These all fuse together in an ambition to create images that are both "good to see and good to think".


I work 100% digitally using an Intuos 4 Wacom tablet. Recently I've also been experimenting with gouache.

Clients: VPRO • Mediahuis • Stew Magazine • Cartoon Movement • Wispfire: Herald
